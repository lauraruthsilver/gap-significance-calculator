Formulas & methodology

Design effect enters as the effective sample size ESS = N / deff (between-sample tests) or as √deff (within-sample tests). Confidence-level multipliers Z: 95% = 1.96, 90% = 1.645, 80% = 1.282.

  • Margin of error (single %): √deff · Z · √(p(1−p)/N) · 100
  • Within one survey (two options): threshold = √deff · Z · √(((p₁+p₂) − (p₁−p₂)²)/(N−1)) · 100
  • Between samples (over time / across countries): threshold = Z · √(p₁(1−p₁)/ESS₁ + p₂(1−p₂)/ESS₂) · 100
  • Difference of differences: per condition VarDiff = (√(p₁(1−p₁)/ESS₁ + p₂(1−p₂)/ESS₂)·100)²; StdErr = √(VarDiff_A + VarDiff_B); threshold = Z · StdErr; compared against |GapA − GapB|.

A difference is significant at a given confidence level when it exceeds that level's threshold. Matches the Global Error Calculator and the trend error workbooks.
